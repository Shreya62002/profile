Trajectory Tracking And Replication Using UAV Github

  • Aimed to develop a controller for a quadcopter to replicate the tracked trajectory of an object.
  • Implemented color filtering and centroid tracking algorithms, and utilized BSpline and RDP algorithms to detect and discretize collected waypoints for optimized object tracking and processing.
  • Stored the waypoints and generated a smooth curve trajectory for 1D, 2D and 3D.
